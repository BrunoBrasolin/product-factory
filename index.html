Sessions

  • First Session
  • Duration: 6 hour
  • Date: November 5
  • View video

In the first session, we will consider design and its understanding as well as differet models of designthinking and the design process, and we will better remember this proess wit various examples.

We will also discuss problem solving techniques together and understand the concept of uder experience and usability.

  • Second Session
  • Duration: 6 hour
  • Date: November 5
  • View video

In the second session, we will discuss service design, first discussing "What is service?"

Then we pratice how to create the right conditions for them in the long run to stay loyal to our product based on the actual needs of our audience and to create solutions that will reduce the hassle of the user in the short run.

We are also going to create a way to measure the performance of our product so that we can ensure the quality of the design solution's performance.

  • Third Session
  • Duration: 6 hour
  • Date: November 5
  • View video

In the third session we will dscuss the most important design phase, wich is the initial research and revie process.

We wll explore user experience research and user research methods.

At the end of the session we will be able t build user personas as well as user scenarios and narrated user stories in the

  • Fourth Session
  • Duration: 6 hour
  • Date: November 5
  • View video

Building experience is one of the key points in the product management and design. But how does this experience come about?

The experienc is made by creating a unique feeling in the mind. In the fourth session, we will learn how to identify user's behavior and motivate them as well as build stimuli to drive the minds of the target audience together.

  • Fifth Meeting
  • Duration: 6 hour
  • Date: November 5
  • View video

It's time to get started. The fifth session is intended to capture the audience's minds and create a new experience for them, like a humorist, sculptor or painter. But with one big difference - and the difference is that we will solve their problems at the same time as targeting users ith product design. The fifth session is actually the beginning of the user experience design process, where we will learn about the concepts of user-centered design and data-driven design.

  • Sixth Session
  • Duration: 6 hour
  • Date: November 5
  • View video

Today, the world is a world of interacton, in the sixth session we understand users trying to find the best way to interact with them and bring the emotions, perceptions, and awareness of the users to the forefront.

We will explore the concept of interaction Design and also explore it in terms of user experience design, usuability and intuitive interface design. In the secont part of the sixth session, we go into marketing and pratice the marketing strategy of digital products together, as well as discussing the appropriate digital marketing toolkit.

  • Seventh Session
  • Duration: 6 hour
  • Date: November 5
  • View video

In the seventh session, we are in the world of product management. We will examine software companies' structures as well as in-team and in-team interactions. In this session, we will look at the duties and characteristcs of product team members as well as identify product strategies and learn about different software development methodologies. And finally, we'll look at a product designer's red line.

  • Eight Session
  • Duration: 6 hour
  • Date: November 5
  • View video

In this session, we will get our hands into the color, learn the basics of user interface design with colored hands, and learn about various interface design tools. We also explore the concepts and fundamentals of iconography and learn about testing the usability of icon design and finally learn te process of visualization in digital products from idea to implementation.

  • Ninth Session
  • Duration: 6 hour
  • Date: November 5
  • View video

In the ninth session we will continue to immerse ourselves in the world of color and image, identifying the elements of the interface end learning how to properly represent the data.

We also remotely team up with Moslem Ebrahimi, a typeface designer, and look at the typography of digital products from the percepctive of someone who has had a lietime of concern of typefaces and Persian fonts.

  • Tenth Meeting
  • Duration: 6 hour
  • Date: November 5
  • View video

In the 10th session we wil start with the software and together we wil learn what design software capabilities are and what ideais can be made with them and that they are not as complex as the cockpit!

We will also discuss problem solving techniques together and understand the concept of uder experience and usability
